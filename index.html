Getting to Split

Split Airport (SPU) · Split, Croatia

Good news, there are direct flights to Split. On a budget? Fly to Zagreb (30–50% cheaper), then take a $20, 5-hour bus to Split.

Split → Vis Ferry

Split Ferry Terminal · Split, Croatia

You'll take a ferry from Split to reach the island. Two options below. Aim to arrive in Vis before 7 PM Monday for Welcome Drinks.

Catamaran: 1 hr 20 min · $9
Sun 7:30 PM – 8:40 PM  |  Mon 3:00 PM – 4:10 PM

Sun 11:00 AM / 7:30 PM  |  Mon 11:00 AM / 6:30 PM

Note: the Mon 6:30 PM standard ferry arrives after Welcome Drinks. If arriving Monday, take the 11:00 AM ferry or the 3:00 PM catamaran.

7:00 PM

Welcome Drinks

Lambik Bar & Bistro · Trg Podlože 2, Vis

Help us kick off the festivities with pizza and beer! This is an informal gathering to help you get settled on the island and see some familiar faces.

Dress code: Casual

Transportation: No shuttle for this event. If staying in Vis, it is a 20-minute walk from Vis to Kut.

5:00 PM – 12:00 AM

Ceremony & Reception

Fort George · Šetalište Apolonija Zanelle 19, Vis

The ceremony and reception will be held in Fort George, a 19th-century British fortress perched above the Adriatic, with panoramic views that stretch all the way to Italy.

Dress code: Cocktail Attire

Weather: 65–75°F (venue is outdoors)

Transportation: Shuttle service with pickup spots in Vis and Kut, 3 stops, all within a 5-minute walk. Stay tuned in the WhatsApp group for pickup details.

8:00 – 9:30 AM

Breakfast

Marshal Club Hotel · Ul. Ivana Farolfija 7, Vis

Whether or not you're joining the beach excursion, come join us for a relaxed post-wedding breakfast.

9:30 AM: Car transport leaves from Marshal Club Hotel to Stiniva Beach.

Transportation: No shuttle. Located between Vis and Kut, a 20-minute walk from Vis.

10:00 AM – 1:30 PM

Stiniva Beach Excursion (Optional)

Stiniva Beach · Marinje Zemlje, Vis

If you're up for it, hike a steep 30-minute goat path and take the bus in. Otherwise, a boat will drop you outside the cove and a smaller dinghy will ferry you through.

At ~1:30 PM, shuttles back to Vis depart to catch the 3:30 PM ferry to Split. Luggage can be stored on the bus.

10:00 AM – 12:00 PM

Padel (Optional)

Padel Centar Split · 4. Gardijske Brigade 28, Split

Join us for some beginner-friendly competition in Europe's version of pickleball! We'll be renting all 4 courts. Rackets provided, athletic wear recommended. There will be a viewing area with snacks and drinks.

Transportation: ~20 min taxi/Uber from Old Town Split. No reservation needed; courts are reserved for the group.

3:00 – 6:00 PM

Diocletian's Palace (Optional)

Diocletian's Palace · Grad, Split

Explore Diocletian's Palace, an ancient Roman fortress built at the end of the 3rd century AD as Emperor Diocletian's retirement residence. When Rome later begged him to return to power, he supposedly replied: “If you could see the cabbages I grow with my own hands, you wouldn't ask me to give this up.”

In Game of Thrones, it was used as Daenerys' Throne Room and Dragon Dungeon, and some alleys were filmed as the streets of Meereen.

Transportation: If staying in Split old city, the palace is within 500 meters. Access is free; it's an open-air structure.
